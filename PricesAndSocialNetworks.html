In "The Journey of Cozcatl", we offer several integration and participation options on social networks to improve your gaming experience and connect with other players. These options are designed to provide an enriching social experience. Below are some of the features and options related to social networks:

1. Connect with Friends: Connect your game account with your social media profiles to find and play with friends who also enjoy "The Journey of Cozcatl." Share achievements, challenges and in-game experiences with your social circle.

2. Share Achievements: Option to share your in-game achievements on your social media profiles. Celebrate your successes with friends and let others see your progress in "The Journey of Cozcatl".

3. Invitations to Friends: Invite friends to join you on your adventure. Send invitations through your social networks so that more people join the "El Viaje de Cozcatl" community.

4. Share Multimedia Content: Capture and share your favorite game moments using images, videos or screenshots directly to your social media profiles.

5. Social Challenges: Participate in in-game social challenges and compete with friends or players from around the world. Share your results and follow the classification in real time.

6. Online Community: Join online communities and groups related to "The Journey of Cozcatl" on your favorite social networks. Participate in discussions, share tips and meet other passionate players.

7. Privacy and Custom Settings: We offer privacy and custom settings options so you can control the information you share on your social media profiles. Your privacy is important to us.

8. Support and Updates: Get news, updates and support through our official social media pages. Stay informed about events, patches and new game features.

9. Community Feedback: Use social media to provide feedback, suggestions, and comments about the game. We strive to build an active and responsive community.

Remember that participation in social networks is optional, and you can enjoy "El Viaje de Cozcatl" completely without using these functions. We respect your privacy and work to offer a positive and safe social experience. Join the community and share your adventures in "The Journey of Cozcatl"!
