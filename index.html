History: The Journey of Cozcatl

A curse fell on the town due to heresy, in which there will be darkness for all eternity, night predominates everywhere, they have no escape, strange sounds and visions begin to frighten the townspeople, which causes the high command to take action. the work to recover its precious light, for the sun to return. Therefore, between the priest Mahuizoh and the other priests, they kidnapped certain people chosen by the sun, who sought them as a sacrifice. The story will begin with a warrior who has been on the front lines for a short time called Cozcatl, trained by one of the marked warriors Tlaloc, his father, who is forced to be sacrificed. His son, Cozcatl, embarks on an adventure to find the heart stone and save his father; or fail and lose everything.
