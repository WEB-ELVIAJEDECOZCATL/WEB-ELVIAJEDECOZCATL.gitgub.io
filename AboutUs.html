ABOUT THE VIDEO GAME

In the next section we will show a detailed description of each character for your knowledge and the existing maps

Characters


Cozcatl



Description: He is a young warrior who had been on the front lines for a short time. His youth and fervor set him apart from others, but his inexperience is overshadowed by iron determination and deep filial love. As he moves forward with agility, his eyes reflect the spark of an indomitable spirit.

Motivation: Cozcatl's driving force is his unwavering love for his father. The news that his father is destined to be sacrificed has lit a burning flame in his heart, prompting him to defy the odds and face formidable enemies in the hopes of saving his loved one.


Xoxhitl



Description: Cozcatl adventure companion. Xoxhitl is a fearless companion of Cozcatl, a warrior with an indomitable heart and sharp cunning. His presence on the battlefield brings a vibrant energy and tactical skill that perfectly complements Cozcatl's determination. With a mix of grace and ferocity, Xoxhitl becomes an invaluable ally in the quest to save Cozcatl's father.

Motivation: Xoxhitl's motivation is fueled by a deep sense of justice and loyalty towards Cozcatl. Although his connection to the mission may lack the blood tie of Cozcatl, his determination to confront injustice and protect those he considers family is as strong as any blood tie.


Tlaloc



Description: Father of Cozcatl, there was one of those marked by the sun to be sacrificed. Tlaloc, the venerable father of Cozcatl, is a man marked by years of wisdom and the experience of leading his people. His imposing figure reflects not only his physical strength, but also the burden of responsibility he carries as a leader and protector of his community. Tlaloc finds himself at the crossroads of his destiny, marked by the sun for a sacrifice that threatens to extinguish the flame of his legacy.

Wisdom: Tlaloc is the bearer of wisdom accumulated over the years. Their advice and teachings have been essential for the growth and survival of the tribe. As a leader, he has guided his people through challenges, celebrations, and times of change.


Priest Mahuizoh



Description: Priest Mahuizoh, a devout believer in the deities of the sun, is a man of deep spirituality and connection with the divine. His presence in the community is synonymous with religious wisdom and unwavering devotion. Dressed in ceremonial vestments adorned with solar symbols, Mahuizoh is the spiritual guide in charge of interpreting celestial signs and ensuring harmony between the gods and the people.

Challenge: Despite his strong faith, Mahuizoh also faces internal challenges in leading his community through times of change and sacrifice. His role as divine intermediary requires a delicate balance between the will of the deities and the needs of his people.
